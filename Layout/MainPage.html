The Headman's Heritage

The Rumah Penghulu Abu Seman was built in stages between 1910 and the early 1930s. Owned by the local headman of Mukim Bagan Samak, Bandar Baharu, Kedah. Originally located in Kampung Sungai Kechil, this traditional Malay house stands as a testament to the rich architectural heritage and cultural traditions of the region.
